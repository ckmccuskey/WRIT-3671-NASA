Effectiveness of the Emblems
Looking Back

Looking back, each of these mission crews did well in designing their mission emblem. These patches were symbolic of so many things: the crews’ objectives, NASA’s goals, and the intentions of the U.S. They weren’t only for NASA, but for the world in a time of challenges and discoveries. The emblems described the missions pictorially and gave all those watching something to embrace.

Looking Now and Into the Future

Moving forward, these emblems have just as much of an impact as they did 50 years ago. They capture the essence of each mission in a way that is hard to achieve. The ethos, logos, and pathos of the Apollo 1, 11, and 17 mission emblems continue to provide historical insight and vision to the world surrounding NASA and will for generations to come.
